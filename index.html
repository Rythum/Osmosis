An academia forum for the students

We are the first ever initiative of IIT Guwahati that specifically targets the academic related problems.A community forum to aid in academics of the students, by enabling content sharing, providing discussions platforms, maintaining a central database and access to mentors.

Our Vision

To maintain a centralized platform for an entire campus with a maintained database, curated list of resources and an academic specific QnA platform.

Provide Resources

Consisting of study materials assembled over years

Discussion Forum

Ask whenever you need or surf through the past discussions

Mentorship

A group of seniors of the same campus for better guidance.
